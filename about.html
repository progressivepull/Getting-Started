Inner Conscience Statement



Welcome back! Here, we focus on educational stories that not only inform but also entertain. Imagine a world where we invest as much in education as we do in the military-industrial complex educational complex powered by major companies, funded by the Department of Education (DE), to build tools and resources for learning.

The storyline here centers on a psychiatrist working with AI in the future. You'll follow his day-to-day life as he tackles various missions for the U.S. government as a federal employee in the Department of IT (DIT). In this future, the internet has evolved beyond imagination, connecting everyone and everything. This interconnected web allows us to explore the fourth dimensions of time and even fifth dimensions, to access different universes.

When you feel inspired, that inspiration might just be a signal from the future, intercepted by your mind. In this world, the line between humans and machines blurs as people can upload their consciousness to the internet and choose to exist either as flesh and blood or in robotic form, depending on the environment of the planet they're on. Humans become trans physical race living on different reality planes.

This website is a gateway to a universe where people can explore different roles, remain anonymous, and contribute to open-source projects. It's a blend of fantasy and education, where you can learn and create while engaging in imaginative storytelling. We're building something fun, educational, and creative together. Welcome to the journey!
